I’ve been doing photography since high school when it was introduced to me through a club. I had owned a camera before, but that was when I really picked it up as an active hobby. I’m a very casual photographer, occasionally going out and especially when I travel. I’m not a very well practicing one though, with little patience for setup or waiting for lighting. Instead I just take opportunistically rather than planning shoots. Some of my work has been published, as well as postcards and even advertising material for a small business. If you want to use any of the photos, feel free to contact me.
